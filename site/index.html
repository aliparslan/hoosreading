Inclusive Reading, Tailored for You

Welcome to our community at Hoos Reading, where accessibility meets personalization. As co-founders, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to explore the world of reading at their own pace. Our journey began with a simple quiz to determine your reading level and has since evolved into an intuitive Chrome extension. This tool not only assesses your proficiency but also adapts any text to match your comfort level, making reading a breeze. It's not just about understanding better—it's about connecting more deeply with every word. With Hoo’s Reading, we're committed to building a space where every member of our diverse community can grow, learn, and enjoy the pleasure of reading.
